Automatic Irrigation with Backup

Smart irrigation system with energy autonomy

Context

Personal project aimed at automating the watering of a small urban vegetable garden while ensuring backup power in case of power outage, in an approach of water economy and autonomy.

Problem

Commercial automatic irrigation systems generally do not have backup power and water at fixed intervals, regardless of actual soil moisture.

Solution

Development of an embedded system based on Arduino: Arduino Uno + capacitive moisture sensor, 5V relay for 12V solenoid valve, 18650 lithium-ion battery (3.7V, 2600mAh) + TP4056 charging module, DC-DC converter (step-up), decision algorithm: watering only if humidity < threshold for 2h.

Tech stack: C++ (Arduino IDE), analog management, PWM control.

Impact

  • 40% reduction in water consumption (vs manual watering)
  • 12h battery autonomy in case of power outage
  • Total cost: ~€25
  • System operational for 8 months without failure
