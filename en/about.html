Who Am I?

I am Benjamin Reuland, an industrial engineering student specializing in electronics and embedded systems at the Haute École de la Province de Liège (HEPL). Motivated and versatile, I possess both technical skills related to my studies and solid field experience through various student jobs.

Passionate about innovative technologies, I develop strong skills in electronic design, embedded programming, and hardware-software integration. Curious, rigorous, and project-oriented, I seek to apply my knowledge in demanding technical environments such as IoT, robotics, or automation.
